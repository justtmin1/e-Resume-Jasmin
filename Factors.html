FACTORS THAT LEAD TO DRINKING ALCOHOL


There are many factors that can contribute to alcohol addiction, including genetics, environment, and personal experiences.

  • Genetics: Research has shown that there is a genetic predisposition to alcohol addiction.
  • Environment: People who are exposed to alcohol at a young age or who have a family history of alcohol abuse are more likely to develop an addiction.
  • Personal experiences: People who have experienced trauma or who have mental health problems are also at an increased risk of developing an alcohol addiction.
