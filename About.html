WHAT IS ALCOHOL?


Alcohol is a psychoactive substance that can have a significant impact on our physical and mental health. It is important to be aware of the effects of alcohol in order to make informed decisions about its use.

  • Alcohol is a depressant that slows down the central nervous system.
  • Alcohol can impair judgment, coordination, and reaction time.
  • Alcohol can increase the risk of accidents, injuries, and violence.
  • Chronic alcohol use can lead to health problems such as liver damage, heart disease, and cancer.
